05 · Value-creation upside

The growth levers

LEVER 01

Activate the Winchester Suite

A third Marloth key currently carrying zero channel nights. Listing it adds inventory at near-zero incremental fixed cost.

LEVER 02

Shift to direct bookings

~R98k of commission leaked at 18% on forward bookings. A live booking-to-WhatsApp pipeline (7 languages) recovers margin on every direct conversion.

LEVER 03

Big Bay short-stay conversion

The lease ends Oct 2026. Converting to holiday-letting under the African Sky brand unlocks the same precinct economics the R4.695m comp demonstrates.

LEVER 04

Cut the cancellation drag

A 41% Booking.com cancellation rate erodes realised occupancy; deposit and rate-rule tightening protects the forward book.
